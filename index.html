Trial And Terror

For a small square came hard times. Life became unbearable in the home world of the figures, each trying to offend, but all because it was smaller than the others in size. The hero could not tolerate such an attitude and decided to leave far away to other worlds. But it was not easy, you have to go through the labyrinth to dive into the portal. In the dark corridors insect mutants swarm. They fly in groups of different numbers, without stopping. You need to select the moment in Trial And Terror to break through their swarm without facing. The hero moves with the help of the arrows to the first obstacle, without stopping. -Game-game
